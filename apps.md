## ExpandableExerciseTimer

A custom workout timer for iOS, macOS, and visionOS. Build routines by mixing time-based and rep-based exercises, dial in rest periods between sets, and get audio alerts when it's time to move on. Born out of frustration with the quirks of browser-based timers, it's grown into a full-featured tool I use every day — for workouts *and* as a slow-eating timer.

[![Download on the App Store](https://tools.applemediaservices.com/api/badges/download-on-the-app-store/black/en-us?size=250x83)](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/expandableexercisetimer/id6756544631)

## Bodyweight WorkoutRandomizer

Can't decide what to do today? This app takes the decision fatigue out of bodyweight training — pick your exercises, set your work and rest intervals, and let it shuffle the rest. Great for days when you want variety without the planning overhead. Grew out of a Zone 2 cardio workaround I built when my ankle was acting up and has been evolving ever since.

- 🧪 [TestFlight beta](https://testflight.apple.com/join/FfJyugX3)
- 💻 [GitHub repo](https://github.com/bigdoodr/WorkoutRandomizer)
- 🍎 App Store — coming soon

## CursorIlluminator

A macOS utility that puts a spotlight on your cursor, making it easy to find on big screens and multi-monitor setups. Simple, lightweight, and does exactly one thing well. Due to macOS sandbox restrictions, it can't live in the App Store — but it's free and open source on GitHub.

- 💻 [GitHub repo](https://github.com/bigdoodr/CursorIlluminator)
- 📦 [Download v1.0.0](https://github.com/bigdoodr/CursorIlluminator/releases/tag/v1.0.0)
- *(Not available on the App Store due to sandbox restrictions)*
