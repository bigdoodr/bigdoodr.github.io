I'm a "I don't really care what you call me" guy.

I'm a TTRPG guy. I'm a fitness guy. I'm a [DansGaming](https://www.twitch.tv/dansgaming) guy.

I'm a Star Wars guy. I'm a horror movies guy. I'm a Halloween guy — not just the movie, the whole season, the whole vibe.

I'm a baby spinach salad guy (pile on the veggies, top it with raspberry vinaigrette). I'm a trail mix guy. I'm a granola-with-banana guy. I'm a Smoothie King guy. I'm a coffee guy. I try to eat smart and healthy — but I'm absolutely, unashamedly, a sweet tooth guy.

I'm a "when I'm at work, I work; when I'm home, I'm home" guy. That line doesn't blur if I can help it.

And I'm a guy who tries to live by a few things people smarter than me have said:

> *"Do the best you can until you know better. Then, when you know better, do better."* — Maya Angelou

> *"Don't yuck someone else's yum."* — Kevin Allison

> *"Do justly. Love mercy. Walk humbly."* — Micah 6:8

> *"Dare greatly."* — Lindsey Vaughn

> *"The divinity in me bows to the divinity in you."* — Hindi teaching
