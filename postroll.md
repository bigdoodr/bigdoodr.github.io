# /postroll

A running list of blog posts that I've found interesting, impactful, or just worth remembering. This list is small right now — I wish I'd been keeping track all along — but it'll grow.

---

**[The Three Paragons of IT: Chidi, Ted & Jules](https://tombridge.com/2021/07/08/the-three-paragons-of-it-chidi-ted-jules/)** — Tom Bridge

A MacAdmins Conference talk framing three pop culture characters — Chidi Anagonye, Ted Lasso, and Jules Winnfield — as archetypes for IT virtues. It's a clever and genuinely useful lens for thinking about what good IT work looks like. The slides are worth a read even if you never watch the video.

---

**[End of Year IT Reflections](https://tombridge.com/2025/12/01/end-of-year-it-reflections/)** — Tom Bridge

A custom set of 20 end-of-year reflection questions tailored specifically for IT professionals. Less navel-gazing, more practically useful than most year-in-review frameworks. I return to this one.
