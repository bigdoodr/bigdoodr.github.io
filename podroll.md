I listen to a *lot* of podcasts. My full list is [over on PocketCasts](https://lists.pocketcasts.com/eb76f2a2-78fa-4568-83da-6cb06bd3c55b), but here are some highlights worth calling out.

## 📰 News & Current Events

- **Up First from NPR** — My morning briefing. Three stories, fifteen minutes, done.
- **Consider This from NPR** — A little more depth on the day's big story.
- **Pod Save America** — Political commentary from former Obama staffers. Opinionated but sharp.
- **The Weekly Show with Jon Stewart** — Jon Stewart is still Jon Stewart. That's all you need to know.

## 🕵️ Investigative & True Crime

- **Serial** — The one that started it all.
- **In The Dark** — Meticulous, important investigative journalism.
- **Bear Brook** — One of the best true crime podcasts ever made.
- **Slow Burn** — Deep dives into pivotal moments in recent American history.
- **Criminal** — Crime stories told with humanity and nuance.

## 👻 Horror & the Supernatural

- **Lore** — Folklore, history, and the dark corners of both. Right in my wheelhouse.
- **Spooked** — True supernatural stories, told by the people who lived them.
- **Unobscured** — Aaron Mahnke goes deep on a single dark historical topic per season.

## 🎙️ Storytelling & Audio Drama

- **This American Life** — A classic for a reason.
- **Snap Judgment** — Vivid, cinematic storytelling.
- **RISK!** — True stories people never thought they'd share out loud. Brave and often hilarious.
- **LeVar Burton Reads** — Short fiction, read beautifully. LeVar Burton could read a grocery list and make it compelling.

## 🧠 Ideas & Curiosity

- **Search Engine** — PJ Vogt asks questions that are hard to stop thinking about.
- **Science Vs** — Takes on viral claims and hot topics with actual data.
- **Throughline** — NPR traces the roots of today's big stories back through history.
- **Your Undivided Attention** — On technology, attention, and what we're doing to our brains.

## ✝️ Faith & Meaning

- **On Being with Krista Tippett** — Deep, generous conversations about what it means to be human.
- **The Liturgists Podcast** — For people who have questions their church couldn't answer.
- **Wild Card with Rachel Martin** — Setup as a game, talks get deep real quick, in a most delightful way.

## 😄 Comedy & Conversation

- **Wait Wait... Don't Tell Me!** — NPR's weekly news quiz. Genuinely funny every time.
- **Wiser Than Me with Julia Louis-Dreyfus** — Julia talks to older, wiser women. Warm and wonderful.
- **Where Everybody Knows Your Name** — Ted Danson and Woody Harrelson being delightful together.
