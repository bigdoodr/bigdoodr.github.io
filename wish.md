# /wish

Not a gift list. A world list.

---

**I wish the world were more like Ted Lasso** — where the people who oppose you are jerks, maybe, but not evil. Where redemption is always on the table and nobody is beyond being reached.

**I wish we meet the goals of the Paris Agreement** — and that future generations inherit a planet that still has room for wonder.

**I wish every person knew they were loved and didn't feel alone** — not as a platitude, but as a lived reality. That no one goes to sleep at night believing nobody would notice if they were gone.

**I wish I didn't make assumptions about others.**
