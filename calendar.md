These are dates from movies, TV shows, and culture that I celebrate or observe throughout the year.

## March

**March 10** - [Mario Day](https://www.mariowiki.com/MAR10_Day) - Celebrating Nintendo's iconic plumber (MAR10 = Mario)

**March 14** - [Pi Day](https://www.piday.org/) - Celebrating the mathematical constant π (3.14)

## April

**April 8** - [Rex Manning Day](https://www.empireonline.com/movies/features/rex-manning-day-empire-records-explained/) - From the cult classic film Empire Records

## May

**May 4** - [Star Wars Day](https://www.starwars.com/star-wars-day) - May the Fourth be with you
