# /nope

Not a page about judging what other people are into — you do you. This is just an honest list of things that aren't for me.

**Sushi.** I know. I know. But no.

**Anything touching my eyeballs.** Contact lenses, eye drops, someone gesturing too close to my face — irrational? Maybe. But the heebie jeebies are very real and very non-negotiable.

**Unstructured social situations.** Small talk, mingling, off-the-cuff conversations where I'm expected to just... have thoughts on demand? I'm awkward, I'm at a loss for words, and I'm uncomfortable. Put me in front of a room with a prepared presentation, though, and I'm completely fine. Give me a structure and I'll thrive. Take it away and I'm just vibing nervously in the corner.
