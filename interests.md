# /interests

## 👨‍👩‍👧‍👦 Family

Everything else on this page exists in the context of this. My family is my anchor and my reason.

## 💪 Fitness

I don't work out to look a certain way. I work out because I want to *live* — not just exist, but truly live — for as long as possible. I want to be around for my kids, and their kids if they choose to have them. That's the motivation. Every rep, every mile.

## 🎲 TTRPGs

Grown adults sitting around a table playing pretend together. I mean that with complete sincerity and zero irony — it's one of my favorite things. TTRPGs are about imagination, storytelling, and spending time with people you care about. What's not to love?

## ☕ Coffee

Not much to explain here. It's coffee. It's good. Moving on.

## 🌌 Star Wars / Horror Movies / Halloween

There's a thread running through all three of these: I'm drawn to the idea that there's *more* out there than what we can see and understand. Magic. The supernatural. The unknown. Whether it's the Force, a haunted house, or the thin veil of October 31st, I'm here for it.

## ✝️ Faith

Same thread, deeper roots. I find meaning in the mystery, and I try to live by Micah 6:8 — do justly, love mercy, walk humbly. That's the whole thing, really.

## ⚖️ Social Justice

Tied to the Maya Angelou quote I carry with me: *"Do the best you can until you know better. Then, when you know better, do better."* I want to put good into the world and use whatever platform or voice I have to speak for those who have been silenced.

## 🍎 Apple / Mac Ecosystem

I'm deep in the Apple world — enough so that I attend [MacAdmins Conference](https://macadmins.psu.edu/) and build apps for the platform. It's a hobby that scratches a creative and technical itch at the same time.
