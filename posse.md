[POSSE](https://indieweb.org/POSSE) stands for **Publish on your Own Site, Syndicate Elsewhere** — the idea that your website is the source of truth and everything else is a copy. Here's where I syndicate and how.

## 📘 Facebook

For family and friends. The audience here knows me personally, so the tone is warmer and more casual — less "here's a thing I made" and more "hey, thought you'd like this."

## 💼 LinkedIn

For career connections and professional context. The same content gets a more polished, professional framing here.

## 🐘 Mastodon — [@bigdoodr@mastodon.social](https://mastodon.social/@bigdoodr)

For the wider web — or at least the niche, lovely corner of it that uses Mastodon. ¯\\\_(ツ)\_/¯ Tone here is more open and public-facing, aimed at people who don't know me at all yet.

---

The content is often the same across all three — but the flavor changes depending on who's listening.
