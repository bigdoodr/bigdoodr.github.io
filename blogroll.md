These are the blogs, publications, and websites I follow regularly. They inform my thinking, keep me up-to-date, and occasionally make me laugh.

## Tech & Apple

**[Daring Fireball](https://daringfireball.net)** - John Gruber's influential Apple and tech commentary

**[Six Colors](https://sixcolors.com)** - Jason Snell's thoughtful analysis of Apple, technology, and media

**[MacStories](https://www.macstories.net)** - Federico Viticci's in-depth coverage of Apple platforms and productivity

**[MacRumors](https://www.macrumors.com)** - Apple news, rumors, and community discussions

**[Eclectic Light Company](https://eclecticlight.co/category/macs/)** - Howard Oakley's deep technical dives into macOS

**[Michael Tsai's Blog](https://mjtsai.com/blog/)** - Curated links and commentary on Mac and iOS development

**[Mr. Macintosh](https://mrmacintosh.com/blog/)** - Essential resource for Mac IT professionals

**[Scripting OS X](https://scriptingosx.com)** - Armin Briegel's guides for Mac automation and scripting

**[Craig Hockenberry (Furbo)](https://furbo.org)** - iOS/Mac developer insights and commentary

**[Basic Apple Guy](https://basicappleguy.com)** - Thoughtful Apple product reviews and design appreciation

## Mac Admin & IT

**[Mac Admins News](https://macadmins.news)** - Weekly newsletter for Apple IT professionals

**[Tom Bridge](https://tombridge.com)** - Leadership and technical insights for Mac admins

**[Jag's Workshop](https://jagsworkshop.com)** - Practical Mac admin tools and workflows

## Tech News & Analysis

**[Ars Technica](https://arstechnica.com)** - In-depth technology news and analysis

**[Hacker News](https://news.ycombinator.com)** - Tech community news aggregator and discussion

**[Wired](https://www.wired.com)** - Technology, science, and culture reporting

**[Popular Science](https://www.popsci.com)** - Science and technology for the curious

**[Simon Willison's Weblog](https://simonwillison.net)** - AI, databases, and web development insights

## Security & Privacy

**[Electronic Frontier Foundation](https://www.eff.org)** - Digital rights and online civil liberties advocacy

**[CIS Security Advisories](https://www.cisecurity.org/advisory)** - Critical security updates and best practices

## News & Culture

**[NPR News](https://www.npr.org/sections/news/)** - National and international news coverage

**[NPR Music](https://www.npr.org/music/)** - Music discovery, interviews, and performances

**[NPR Culture](https://www.npr.org/sections/culture/)** - Arts, books, film, and cultural commentary

**[Vox Explainers](https://www.vox.com/explainers)** - Context and clarity on complex topics

**[ProPublica](https://www.propublica.org)** - Investigative journalism in the public interest

**[Relevant Magazine](https://relevantmagazine.com)** - Faith, culture, and intentional living

**[WKYU-FM](https://www.wkyufm.org)** - Local public radio station

## Health & Fitness

**[Athlean-X Articles](https://learn.athleanx.com/articles)** - Science-based fitness and training advice from Jeff Cavaliere

**[Nerd Fitness](https://www.nerdfitness.com/start-here/)** - Fitness and health for nerds and everyday people

## Parenting & Family

**[Common Sense Media Articles](https://www.commonsensemedia.org/articles)** - Reviews and advice for raising kids in the digital age

## Comics & Humor

**[xkcd](https://xkcd.com)** - Randall Munroe's webcomic of romance, sarcasm, math, and language

**[Sarah's Scribbles](https://sarahcandersen.com)** - Sarah Andersen's relatable comics about life and anxiety

**[Cat's Cafe Comics](https://www.catscafecomics.com)** - Wholesome comics about animals and friendship
