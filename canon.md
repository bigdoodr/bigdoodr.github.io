This is my personal canon - the books, films, music, games, experiences, and other media that have significantly shaped who I am. It's an ongoing work, capturing the influences that have left lasting marks on my thinking, values, and perspective.

## Books

**[Ragamuffin Gospel](https://www.goodreads.com/book/show/145948.The_Ragamuffin_Gospel) by Brennan Manning** - A transformative look at grace and God's unconditional love that still inspires me, despite my complicated relationship with religion

**[How to Be Perfect](https://www.simonandschuster.com/books/How-to-Be-Perfect/Michael-Schur/9781982159313) by Michael Schur** - The creator of The Good Place explores moral philosophy with humor and accessibility, making ethics feel approachable and relevant

## Films

**[Clue](https://www.imdb.com/title/tt0088930/)** - A perfect comedy that taught me the joy of wordplay, timing, and multiple possibilities

**[The Goonies](https://www.imdb.com/title/tt0089218/)** - Childhood adventure, friendship, and the belief that kids can do extraordinary things

## Music & Albums

**[The Joshua Tree](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/The_Joshua_Tree) by U2** - An album that shaped my musical tastes and introduced me to music as more than entertainment

**[The Beautiful Letdown](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/The_Beautiful_Letdown) by Switchfoot** - Faith, doubt, and hope wrapped in unforgettable melodies

**[Johnny Cash's "Hurt"](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=8AHCfZTRGiI)** - A cover and music video that redefined what it means to reflect on a life lived. Raw, vulnerable, and haunting

## Games

**[Vampire the Masquerade: Bloodlines](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Vampire:_The_Masquerade_%E2%80%93_Bloodlines)** - A flawed masterpiece that showed me games could tell mature, complex stories with genuine moral ambiguity

**[Snatcher](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Snatcher_(video_game)) (Sega CD)** - Hideo Kojima's cyberpunk adventure that demonstrated how games could blend cinematic storytelling with interactive narrative

## Experiences & Events

**Centrifuge and M-Fuge Camps** - Youth camp experiences that shaped my faith and worldview, without the religious baggage (it was there, but I didn't carry it with me)

**XTreme Winter Conferences in Gatlinburg, TN** - Winter retreats that challenged and deepened my spiritual journey as a teenager

**Passport Missions in Daytona, Florida** - A summer of service after my college freshman year that opened my eyes to different ways of living and serving

**Sandy Hook** - A tragedy that fundamentally changed how I think about safety, childhood, and our responsibilities to protect the vulnerable

## Other Media

**[Guns with History](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=1nAfWfF4TjM)** - A video that reframed how I think about gun violence and policy through data and storytelling

**[Three Paragons of IT](https://tombridge.com/2021/07/14/video-posted-for-three-paragons-of-it/) by Tom Bridge** - A presentation that articulated the ethical framework I strive to embody as an IT professional

---

*This is a living document. As I continue to reflect on what has shaped me, I'll add to this list.*
